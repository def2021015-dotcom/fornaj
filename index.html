A letter for you ✦

My dearest,

There are things that words can barely hold — but I want to try. Every time I think of you, something warm settles in my chest, like sunlight finding its way through a window on a quiet morning.

I wrote this not because I had to, but because you deserve to know how much you mean to me. In every small moment and every ordinary day, you make the world softer somehow.

Thank you for being exactly who you are. For your laugh, your patience, your kindness — and even the way you tilt your head when you’re thinking. I notice it all.

I hope whenever you read this, it finds you smiling. And I hope you know — always — that you are so very loved.

With all my heart, Me ♡
